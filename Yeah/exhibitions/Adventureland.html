Adventureland

Campbell Mcconnell
James Lang
Joana Azevedo

Shipton is pleased to present ‘Adventureland’, a group exhibition featuring the work of Campbell Mcconnell, Joana Azevedo & James Lang.

This exhibition recalls the essence of honour, valour, and glory, echoing the adventurous spirit of King Arthur's knights of the Round Table, where bravery outshone fear.

Through a collection that melds myth with the quest for discovery, visitors are challenged to embrace the boldness and ambition that shaped the legends of yesteryear.

The artworks invite viewers to journey through realms where myth and reality intertwine, inspiring a fearless pursuit of greatness.
